Why choose us?

At Sandhills Landscape Services, our mission is to make the Sandhills a better and more vibrant place to live. We all know that there are a lot of pine trees in the Sandhills. This means that there is also a lot of pine needles and other tree debris.

Many people live in fear of leaves and especially pine needles, as they make a blanket over the grass, killing it slowly and downgrading the look of the lawn. This creates space for weeds and other unwanted growth. How are we supposed to prevent this? The best way is to prevent it even before it can happen, and no, we do not mean removing your trees, as they are vital to the eco-system.

We mean landscaping! We offer a range of services, from tree debris and stick removal to well kept lawns, mowed and blown. It doesn't take much to keep a lawn clean, so we don't charge too much either, offering reasonable prices for our jobs.
